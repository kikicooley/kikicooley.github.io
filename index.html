Welcome to Passport Local. You choose your guide. We'll plan your stay. Whether you're in town for a conference, have a long layover, or just don't know what to do or see in the Emerald City, we've got you covered. We'll show you our favorite holes in the wall, the places we haunt, and hidden gems so you can see Seattle like a local.

Who we are and why we're here!

With nearly three decades as Washingtonians spread among us, we're pretty positive we've got a good idea of the best sightseeing, beaches, yoga studios, and coffee. Our mission is share our experiences with you so you can beat the lines, skip the tourist traps, and see the city through our eyes. At Passport Local we're more than just tour guides. We're your new best friends. Let us help you beat the Seattle Freeze.
